About KHIT

A legacy of educational excellence and moral values.

Our History

Kallam Haranadha Reddy Institute of Technology (KHIT) was established in the year 2010 by the Kallam Academy of Educational Society. The society was founded by industrialist Sri Kallam Haranadha Reddy, Chairman of the Kallam Group of Industries.

Situated in the serene environment of Chowdavaram, Guntur, KHIT has rapidly grown into a premier institution for engineering education in Andhra Pradesh. Affiliated to JNTUK Kakinada and approved by AICTE, KHIT is committed to producing industry-ready professionals.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education and research, producing globally competent engineers with ethical values and social responsibility.

Mission

To impart quality education through innovative teaching-learning processes.
To foster research and development culture.
To empower students with leadership skills and professional ethics.
